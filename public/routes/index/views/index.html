How do I get started?

All you have to do is enter a room name. You should see a request at the top of your browser asking to use your webcam. Click ACCEPT or ALLOW and you're ready to go! <br /><br />* You can also go directly to a room, i.e. <b>chatboxx.io/YourRoomHere</b>, and a room will be automatically created for you without having to navigate to the homepage first. <br /><br />* If there are already 6 people in a room or the room has been locked by an occupant, you won't be permitted into a room.

It's not working for me!

Currently, Chatboxx only supports the newest versions of Chrome. It DOES NOT support iOS (iPhone, iPad, etc), Internet Explorer, or Safari browsers. The peer-to-peer technology required doesn't exist on older browsers, nor has Microsoft or Apple taken the time to build the functionality into their software / hardware.

We're working hard to provide support for other platforms, but you should be using the newest versions of Chrome to fully experience the web.
