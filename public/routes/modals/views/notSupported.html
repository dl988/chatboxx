Uh-oh!

It looks like we're unable to access your webcam / screen. Here's a quick trouble-shooting guide.

  • Make sure you're on a desktop or laptop running the latest version of Google Chrome.
  • Currently, mobile devices and other browsers don't support WebRTC, the protocol we use for connecting you to your peers and stream media.
  • A toolbar should pop up near the top of your screen when you click the Video or Screen Share buttons. Make sure you click Accept so we can stream your media to peers.
  • Check the web address bar for the video icon. Click on it and make sure you've selected to share your media with this url.
  • IMPORTANT: If you are unable to Screen Share, open another tab in Google Chrome and enter chrome://flags in the address bar. Find the item that says Enable screen capture support in getUserMedia(). and enable it. You can quickly find this by using CTRL + F and searching for getUserMedia. Restart your browser afterwards.
